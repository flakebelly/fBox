fTape

Multi-Mode Tape Machine - VST3 / AU

Six tape machines spanning the full history of magnetic recording

Overview

fTape emulates six distinct tape machines, each with its own transport characteristics, frequency response, saturation behaviour, and noise profile. The reel animation is driven by the DAW playhead - press play and the reels turn, stop and they stop. Each mode models the complete analogue tape recording chain including frequency-dependent saturation, bias, noise, and mechanical character.

Modes

Home Deck

Quarter-inch reel-to-reel at 3.75 or 7.5 IPS. Warm, lo-fi, characterful.

Cassette

Compact cassette with switchable tape types, Dolby noise reduction, and varispeed pitch.

Multitrack

8-track quarter-inch at 15 IPS with dbx noise reduction, crosstalk, and print-through.

Broadcast

Semi-pro broadcast 1-inch stereo deck. 7.5 or 15 IPS, NAB/IEC EQ, three-head design.

Studio 2"

High-end 2-inch 24-track. 15 or 30 IPS, NAB/CCIR EQ, transformer electronics.

Heritage

Vintage 4-track 1-inch studio deck. IEC EQ, 4-head with sel-sync, permalloy heads.
