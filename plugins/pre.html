fPre

Multi-Mode Preamplifier - VST3 / AU

Six preamplifier topologies from one plugin

Overview

fPre provides six distinct preamplifier circuits, each modelling a different amplification topology. From Class-A transformer warmth to ultra-transparent discrete solid-state, each mode is a complete preamp with its own gain staging, harmonic character, and transient response.

Modes

British

Class-A discrete transformer-coupled preamp with inductor EQ.

American

FET solid-state discrete preamp with 3:1 output transformer tap. Pure preamp, no EQ.

Tube

All-tube pentode preamp with push-pull output and hot bias. Pure preamp, no EQ.

Clean

Ultra-transparent discrete Class-A solid-state. No transformers, capacitor-coupled I/O, switchable impedance.

Germanium

Vintage PNP germanium transistor preamp. Transformer-balanced I/O, temperature drift, even-harmonic warmth.

Console

IC op-amp mixing console channel strip. Switchable iron input transformer, slew-rate limiting, feedback-loop saturation.
