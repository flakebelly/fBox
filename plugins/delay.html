fDelay

Multi-Mode Delay - VST3 / AU

Eight delay modes with switchable GUI per mode

Overview

fDelay provides eight delay modes covering tape echo, digital, analogue bucket-brigade, ping-pong, and more. Each mode has its own DSP engine, its own GUI, and its own sonic character. Like all fBox multi-mode plugins, a single mode switch changes both the sound and the interface.
