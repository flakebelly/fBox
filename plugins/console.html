fBox Console

Virtual Analogue Mixing Console

32-Channel Mixing Console - VST3 + Standalone

fBox Console is a virtual analogue mixing console available as both a VST3 plugin and a standalone desktop application. It provides up to 32 mono channel strips, 8 group buses, 2 stereo aux sends with returns, and a stereo master section - all in a single window.

It is not a collection of unrelated processors. It is one integrated desk. Levels, routing, and gain staging are part of the sound, not an afterthought. The console's non-linear summing stages respond to cumulative signal level, producing density and glue as the mix fills out, just as a real analogue desk does.

Every channel strip includes a switchable preamp with four modes, 8 hosted insert slots for fBox VST3 plugins, a single-knob channel compressor, a 4-band vintage-style EQ, 2 aux sends, pan, mute, solo, and a fader. All parameters are automatable through the console's own built-in multi-pass automation engine or via DAW automation.

The 8 group buses each feature a 3-band EQ with high-pass filter, a VCA bus compressor, 4 insert slots, and dual pan controls. The master section adds an analogue summing amplifier, a vari-mu compressor, a diode limiter, and its own 3-band EQ with HPF and 4 insert slots.

The standalone version includes a 16-track Tape Machine - a multitrack tape simulation modelled on a classic recorder, integrated directly into the console signal path. Load audio files onto channels, mix on the console, and bounce the result - no DAW required.

Every instance of fBox Console generates a unique serial number that seeds subtle hardware tolerances across the buses and master section. No two instances sound exactly alike.

Channel Signal Flow

Trim→ Preamp→ 8x Inserts→ Comp→ 4-Band EQ→ Aux Sends→ Fader→ Pan→ Bus / Master

32 Channel Strips

Each with switchable preamp (Clean, Warm, Hot, Valve), 8 hosted insert slots, channel compressor, 4-band EQ, 2 aux sends, pan, mute, solo, and fader.

8 Group Buses

3-band EQ with HPF, VCA bus compressor, 4 insert slots, and dual pan controls. Route any channel to any bus for stem and group processing.

Master Section

Analogue summing amplifier, vari-mu compressor, diode limiter, 3-band EQ with HPF, 4 insert slots, and stereo metering.

16-Pass Automation

Built-in automation engine with touch recording. Record moves across multiple passes, with section-based workflow. Every parameter is automatable.

Tape Machine (Standalone)

16-track multitrack tape simulation with per-track drive, EQ curves, output drive, and internal amp modelling. Integrated into the console signal path.

VST3 Insert Hosting

Host fBox VST3 plugins in channel, bus, and master insert slots. Per-slot bypass, fault isolation, and full automation support.
