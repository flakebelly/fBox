fBox Console

32-Channel Mixing Console - VST3 / AU / Standalone

A complete virtual analogue mixing desk

Overview

fBox Console is a 32-channel virtual analogue mixing desk. It runs as a VST3 or AU plugin inside your DAW, or as a standalone application. 32 mono channel strips, 8 group buses, 2 stereo aux sends with returns, and a stereo master section - all with analogue-style non-linear summing and per-instance hardware character.

It is not a collection of unrelated processors. It is one integrated desk where levels, routing, and gain staging are part of the sound. The console's non-linear summing stages respond to the combined energy of all channels feeding a bus, exactly as a real analogue summing amplifier does.

Channel Strip

Every channel includes a switchable preamp (Clean, Warm, Hot, Valve), 8 hosted insert slots for fBox plugins, a single-knob opto channel compressor with programme-dependent attack and release, a 4-band vintage-style EQ (high-pass filter, low bell, mid bell, high shelf - all stepped frequencies), 2 aux sends with pre/post switching, pan, mute, solo, and a fader. The preamp is signal-level driven - there is no drive knob. Push the trim harder and the preamp saturates more, just like real hardware.

Bus and Master

The 8 group buses each have a 3-band EQ with high-pass filter, a VCA bus compressor with parallel mix, 4 insert slots, and dual pan controls. The master section adds an analogue-style summing amplifier, a 3-band EQ, a vari-mu compressor with gain reduction metering, and a diode limiter.

Automation Pass System

The console has its own built-in automation engine, completely independent of your DAW's automation. Create unlimited passes and layer them on top of each other. Record fader rides, knob turns, EQ moves, mute presses - every parameter on every channel, bus, and the master section. Each pass is independent: maybe Pass 1 is your fader balance, Pass 2 is EQ tweaks, Pass 3 is panning, Pass 4 is effects sends. Lock the ones you are happy with. Build the mix up over time, exactly like working on a real console.

The system also records every parameter on every hosted insert plugin. Grab a knob on fComp in an insert slot while a pass is recording and the move is captured. Automation never fights you - grab any control during playback and your live movement takes priority until you release it.

Channel Signal Flow

Trim→ Preamp→ 8x Inserts→ Comp→ 4-Band EQ→ Aux Sends→ Fader→ Pan→ Bus / Master
