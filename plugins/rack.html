fBox Rack

Modular Insert Chain - VST3 / AU

A complete channel in a single plugin

Overview

fBox Rack is a modular insert chain that hosts up to 8 VST3 plugins of any kind in series - fBox processors, third-party plugins, or any combination. It includes a built-in preamp with four modes, a console output stage with five modes, and a full mix bus strip with summing, 4-band EQ, VCA compressor with gain reduction metering, and 3-mode saturation.

Think of it as a complete recording channel in a single plugin. Load it on a track and you have a preamp, an 8-slot effects chain, and a mix bus - all in one place with left-to-right signal flow.
