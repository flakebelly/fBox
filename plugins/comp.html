fComp

Multi-Mode Compressor - VST3 / AU

Nine compression engines in one plugin

Overview

fComp puts nine distinct compression engines in a single plugin. Each mode has its own DSP engine, its own GUI, and its own sonic character. Switch between them with a single dropdown - from fast FET peak limiting to smooth optical levelling to transparent VCA bus glue to vintage variable-mu warmth.

Modes

FET

Fast-attack FET peak limiter. Aggressive, punchy, and immediate.

Opto

Photocell-based optical compressor. Smooth, musical, programme-dependent timing.

VCA

VCA bus compressor. Clean, precise, transparent glue for buses and mix.

Vari-Mu

Variable-mu tube compressor. Warm, thick, feedback topology with gentle ratio curves.

PWN

Pulse width modulation broadcast compressor. Dense, loud, broadcast-ready.

Diode Bridge

Germanium diode bridge compressor. Gritty, colourful, feedback topology.

De-Esser

Frequency-selective dynamics. Tames sibilance without dulling the source.

Zener

Zener diode limiter. Three sub-modes for brickwall, soft, and character limiting.

Gate

VCA noise gate, expander, and ducker. Three sub-modes for flexible dynamics control.
