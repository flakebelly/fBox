fSat

Multi-Mode Saturation - VST3 / AU

Eight saturation and harmonic generation engines

Overview

fSat is the dedicated saturation and harmonic generation tool in the fBox ecosystem. Where fPre has saturation as a byproduct of preamp modelling and fTape has it as part of tape emulation, fSat is what you load when you deliberately want to shape harmonics on a signal. Eight modes from gentle transformer iron warmth to aggressive fuzz.

Modes

Silk

Transformer iron saturation. Even-order harmonics, the gentlest onset. Weight control: Light, Medium, Heavy.

Glow

Triode tube saturation. Even-order harmonics, programme-dependent compression, tube bloom. Bias: Cool, Norm, Hot.

Push

Transistor saturation. Odd-order edge with a pre-saturation tilt. Tone: Dark, Neutral, Bright.

Diode

Germanium and silicon clipping diodes. Switchable Ge/Si type with post-clip tone sweep.

Clip

Precision clipper with adjustable ceiling and knee. Expanded GUI with scrolling waveform scope and transfer curve.

Rect

Rectifier saturation.

Crush

Bit depth and sample rate reduction.

Fuzz

Cascaded vintage fuzz stages.
