fEQ

Multi-Mode Equaliser - VST3 / AU

Six EQ personalities in one plugin

Overview

fEQ provides six complete equaliser engines covering every tonal task a mix or mastering session demands. Each mode has its own DSP engine, its own GUI skin, and its own sonic character - from surgical digital precision to warm vintage inductor EQ.

Modes

Hybrid

7-band digital/analogue switchable EQ. Precise control with optional analogue saturation.

Passive

Inductor and tube programme EQ with the classic boost-and-attenuate trick.

Air

Dual-parallel high-frequency EQ with iron transformer.

Desk

British console 4-band parametric. Musical, broad strokes.

Surgical

500-series 3-band proportional Q. Tighter Q at higher gain for precision cuts.

Bus

Passive tube mastering EQ for gentle broad shaping on buses and master.
