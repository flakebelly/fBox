fMod

Multi-Mode Modulation Processor - VST3 / AU

Eight classic modulation circuits

Overview

fMod provides eight modulation engines covering every classic modulation effect - from lush bucket-brigade chorus to through-zero flanging to dual-rotor Leslie emulation. Each mode has its own DSP engine, GUI layout, and per-instance hardware tolerances. Tempo sync is available on six modes.

Modes

Chorus

BBD bucket-brigade with compandor noise reduction. 1-3 voices with even phase spread.

Flanger

Through-zero capable with signed feedback from -95% to +95%. Deep, metallic sweeps.

Phaser

Cascaded all-pass with JFET sweep. 4, 6, 8, or 12 stages.

Tremolo

Bias tube and optical photocell sub-modes. Stereo width control and sidechain input.

Vibrato

BBD pitch modulation with rise envelope and latch for mechanical pitch effects.

Rotary

Dual-rotor Leslie with independent horn and drum speeds, Doppler effect, and cabinet EQ.

Ring Mod

4-diode germanium ring modulator with polyBLEP carrier. Sidechain replaces carrier.

Univibe

Photocell 4-stage phaser with incandescent lamp model and transistor drive.
