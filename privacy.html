Privacy Policy

fBox Audio - fboxaudio.com

Last updated: March 2026

Who We Are

fBox Audio is operated by flakebelly, a sole trader based in Scotland, UK. We develop and sell audio software plugins under the fBox Audio brand.

Contact: fboxaudio@gmail.com

What Data We Collect

When you make a purchase or download a demo version, your data is handled by our merchant of record. The data they collect in order to process your transaction may include:

  • Your name and email address
  • Payment information (handled and stored securely by the merchant of record — we never see your full payment details)
  • Your IP address and country, for tax purposes

Our merchant of record is responsible for the processing and storage of your personal and payment data. Their own privacy policy applies to all data they collect on our behalf.

Mailing List

If you sign up to the fBox Audio mailing list, we collect and store your email address. This data is collected directly by fBox Audio and stored by us — it is not shared with or processed by any third-party mailing list provider.

Your email address will be used to send you updates about fBox Audio products, including new releases, updates, and other news. We will not share your email address with any third party or use it for any purpose other than fBox Audio communications.

You can unsubscribe from the mailing list at any time by contacting fboxaudio@gmail.com. Upon unsubscribing, your email address will be deleted from our mailing list records.

By signing up to the mailing list, you agree to the fBox Audio Terms & Conditions.

Cloudflare

This website is served through Cloudflare, which provides security, performance, and content delivery services. Cloudflare may process certain technical data including IP addresses as part of their service. This data is used for security and performance purposes only. Cloudflare's privacy policy can be found at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

How We Use Your Data

The personal data we hold is used for the following purposes only:

  • Your email address from our merchant of record is used for delivering your licence and any essential product communications such as update notifications or critical bug notices
  • Your email address from the mailing list, if you have signed up, is used to send you updates about fBox Audio products, new releases, and other news

We do not sell, share, or rent your personal data to any third party.

Licence Delivery

When you purchase an fBox Audio product, your licence key is delivered to you via email. Software downloads are hosted by our merchant of record. Demo versions are available as a free download via our merchant of record with no payment required.

When you activate a plugin, it sends your licence key and a machine identifier to our server at api.fboxaudio.com. This data is used solely to validate your licence and manage activations. An encrypted licence file is then stored locally on your machine — no internet connection is required after initial activation.

fBox Audio may use your email address to deliver licence-related communications. You will not be added to any marketing list without your consent.

Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data held about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request restriction of processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at fboxaudio@gmail.com. For data held by our merchant of record, you should contact them directly via their privacy policy.

Data Retention

We retain your email address from our merchant of record for as long as your licence is active, for the purpose of delivering essential product communications. You may request deletion at any time by contacting fboxaudio@gmail.com.

If you have signed up to our mailing list, we retain your email address until you unsubscribe. You can unsubscribe at any time by contacting fboxaudio@gmail.com, and your email address will be deleted from our mailing list records.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of fboxaudio.com following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at fboxaudio@gmail.com.
