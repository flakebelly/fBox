Demo Versions

Every fBox Audio product is available as a free demo version, downloadable at no cost and with no payment information required.

Demo versions are fully functional and work in any VST3-compatible DAW. They include a noise burst every 30 seconds to distinguish them from purchased versions. All features, signal processing, and plugin behaviour are otherwise identical to the purchased version.

We strongly recommend downloading and testing the demo version before purchasing. For fBox Console specifically, we recommend evaluating the demo first — while the Console works in all VST3 hosts, the ability to route multiple tracks simultaneously into the Console depends on your DAW's multichannel routing capabilities, which vary between hosts.

Demo versions do not expire. You can use them to evaluate fBox Audio products for as long as you need before deciding to purchase.
